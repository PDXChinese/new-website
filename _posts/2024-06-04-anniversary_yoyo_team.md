一年前，一群孩子在教练凯莉的指导下成立了空竹队。他们积极参与社区活动，成为OCC推广活动的重要一部分。他们的空竹表演已经成为学校和社区中心的热门活动。

A group of kids started the YoYo team under Coach Callie one year ago. They have actively participated in community events as part of OCC's outreach efforts. Their YoYo performances have become a popular activity at schools and community centers.
