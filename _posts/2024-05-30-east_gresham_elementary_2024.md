周四，社区与 East Gresham Elementary School 的家庭一起参加了他们的多元文化节。我们提供了精彩的表演并设立了一个手工摊位，整个活动期间都非常受欢迎。

Last Thursday, the community joined the families at East Gresham Elementary School for its multicultural festival. We provided performances and hosted a craft table, which was very popular throughout the event.
